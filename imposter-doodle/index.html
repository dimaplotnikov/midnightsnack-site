Imposter Doodle

One phone, one secret word — but the imposter must fake it!

Gather 3–10 friends around one phone. Everyone adds one stroke to the same doodle — except the imposter, who only knows the category and has to fake it. Debate the drawing, vote out the fake artist… but a caught imposter can still steal the win by guessing the word!

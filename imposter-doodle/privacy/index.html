Privacy Policy

Imposter Doodle · Midnight Snack Games · Last updated: July 1, 2026

Short version: Imposter Doodle is a pass-the-phone party game you play offline. We don’t make you create an account and we never ask for your name, email, or contacts. We use a few standard third-party services to process purchases, understand how the game is used, measure our marketing, and fix crashes. The Game does not currently show in-app ads, and we do not sell your personal information.

Who we are

Imposter Doodle (“the Game”) is developed and published by Midnight Snack Games (“we”, “us”), the data controller for the purposes of this policy. Contact: hello@midnightsnack.games. Our postal contact details are available on request by email.

Information we collect

  • Player names you type for a game session are stored only on your device to run the round. They are not transmitted to us.
  • Game settings & local progress (chosen language, selected word pack) are stored on your device. Purchase entitlements may also be validated and restored through RevenueCat (see below).
  • Purchase information. If you buy a word pack or the “unlock all” bundle, the purchase is processed by Apple or Google. We use RevenueCat to validate, store, and restore purchase entitlements; it processes a randomly generated app user ID, your purchase history, and device/OS identifiers.
  • Usage & diagnostics (Google Firebase / Google Analytics). App events (e.g., a round started/finished, a pack previewed or purchased), app version, device model, operating system, language, coarse region, crash logs, and app-generated/device identifiers.
  • Marketing attribution (AppsFlyer). If you install the Game after seeing an ad or a creator link, we collect the install source and, where permitted, your advertising identifier, to measure our own campaigns.

We do not knowingly collect real names, email addresses, precise location, photos, or contacts.

How we use it

  • To provide the Game and process, restore, and manage in-app purchases.
  • To understand how the Game is used and improve it.
  • To measure and improve our own advertising and creator campaigns.
  • To diagnose crashes and keep the Game stable and secure.

Legal bases (EEA/UK)

Where GDPR applies, we rely on: performance of a contract for providing the Game and processing/restoring purchases; your consent for analytics and marketing attribution that use your advertising identifier (requested via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency where required) — which you may withdraw at any time; and our legitimate interests in crash diagnostics, security/fraud prevention, and improving the Game.

Service providers & international transfers

We share limited data with providers who process it on our behalf: Apple App Store and Google Play (purchases), RevenueCat (purchase management), Google Firebase / Google Analytics (analytics & crash reporting), and AppsFlyer (marketing attribution, including install source and advertising identifier where permitted). For EEA/UK users, transfers outside the EEA/UK rely on adequacy decisions or Standard Contractual Clauses with supplementary measures where required. Each provider handles data under its own privacy policy.

Selling & sharing of personal information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share advertising identifiers with AppsFlyer to measure our own advertising where permitted; where California law (CCPA/CPRA) treats sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising as a “sale” or “share,” California residents may opt out by emailing hello@midnightsnack.games and by disabling ad tracking in your device settings.

Your choices & rights

  • On iOS, we ask for your permission before any tracking that uses your advertising identifier; you can decline, and you can change this anytime in your device settings.
  • You can reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings.
  • Because Imposter Doodle is an offline mobile app (not a website), it does not respond to browser “Do Not Track” signals; use your device’s ad-tracking controls to limit tracking.
  • Depending on where you live (e.g., EEA/UK under GDPR, California under CCPA/CPRA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your data, to object to certain processing, and to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
  • To exercise a right, email hello@midnightsnack.games with your request and, if available, your device or app user ID; we may need information to verify your request.

Children

The Game is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected personal information, including persistent identifiers, from a child under 13 without required consent, we will delete or de-identify it.

Data retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear its data. Purchase records are kept as long as needed to provide and restore purchases and to meet tax/accounting obligations. Analytics, attribution, and crash data are retained only as long as needed for measurement, fraud prevention, and debugging, after which they are deleted or aggregated.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date on this page.

Contact

Request Deletion of Your Data

Imposter Doodle · Midnight Snack Games · Last updated: July 2, 2026

Imposter Doodle (by Midnight Snack Games) has no accounts and stores your game content only on your device. A small amount of analytics, crash, attribution, and purchase-entitlement data is processed by our service providers — and you can ask us to delete it at any time.

How to request deletion (step by step)

  1. Write an email to hello@midnightsnack.games with the subject “Imposter Doodle — Data deletion request”.
  2. Tell us the platform you play on (iOS or Android). If you made purchases, mention that too — it helps us locate the purchase-entitlement record.
  3. We will confirm your request and complete the deletion with our service providers within 30 days.

Additionally: data stored on your device (player names you typed, settings, unlocked packs cache) is removed immediately when you uninstall the app. In-app, you can also turn off analytics anytime in Settings → Analytics.
